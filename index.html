About

After getting stuck in Australia during the pandemic, I spent five years working as an indie filmmaker, directing two shorts and a feature film, "I Fall to Pieces" (currently in post). After directing "Pieces", I realized the questions I was asking-about audiences, engagement, and impact—could be better understood with data than just pure intuition.

Now I'm studying Data Science at the University of Michigan, adding a technical layer to my experience. I've worked in content marketing, building strategies driven by user engagement data. As Runway's first Student Ambassador at Michigan, I'm helping creators understand how AI tools can amplify their creative vision.

I'm looking for roles where data meets storytelling, whether that's growth, marketing, product management, or building tools that help people tell stories of their own.
